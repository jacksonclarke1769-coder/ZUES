# User Trading Profile

## Primary Instrument & Timeframe
- **Primary**: XAUUSD (gold) — 5-minute entry chart
- **Secondary**: NQ (Nasdaq-100 futures, CME) — 5-minute entry chart (currently in validation phase)
- **Sessions**: London + New York (European morning + US morning)

## Trading Style
- **Framework**: ICT (Inner Circle Trading) smart-money concepts
- **Key mechanics**: Liquidity sweeps, break of structure, displacement, fair-value gaps
- **Entry type**: Order Template Entry (OTE); structural break below recent low
- **Stop convention**: ~6 points (XAUUSD), ~41–67 points (NQ)
- **Target convention**: 10–15 points (XAUUSD), ~2R fixed (NQ)
- **Philosophy**: Repeatable statistical edge, not get-rich-quick; data-tested, rule-based

## Account & Goal
- **Goal**: Rule-based strategies for funded accounts (FTMO, Topstep)
- **Account size (funded)**: Topstep 50K (NQ micros), FTMO varies
- **Risk tolerance**: Conservative on funded accounts (≤3 MNQ); aggressive on Combine (4–5 MNQ)
- **Timeframe for success**: 30–60d live micro forward test for validation; 6–12 months to first payout on funded account

## Strategy Selection Criteria
- **Logic score**: ≥7/10 required before building
- **Backtest min**: ≥30 trades (≥50 for risk certification)
- **Realism**: Commission + slippage baked in; no optimistic tests
- **Refinement limit**: Max 2 loops per strategy without explicit user approval
- **Risk gate**: FAIL from risk-funded-agent blocks deployment, period
